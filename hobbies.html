My Hobbies

Going to the beach is one of Chris’ favorite hobbies, connecting with nature and being able to relax resets his mind. While at the beach he loves listen to Alanis Morissette’s hits, because her songs are meaningful.

While he is at home he loves playing with his dogs and taking care of his plants, he gets satisfaction of nourishing something and watch it grow. He likes the fascinating benefits of nature overall.

Trying new food is always in his to-do list, whether staying at home ordering take-out from a new place while enjoying a good comedy show, or cooking a new recipe found online.
